About

Sumo robots are robots designed to fight each other like sumo wrestlers. They compete in a ring and must attempt to push their opponent out of bounds. They are usually autonomous and rely on infrared or ultrasonic sensors to navigate.

They come in a wide range of sizes, ranging from lightweight plastic robots to 20lb steel behemoths. They are the main event at competitions all around the world, and they have inspired enthusiasts of all ages to learn more about robotics and engineering.
