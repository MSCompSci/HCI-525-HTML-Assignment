Sumo Robot Competitions

National Robotics Challenge

The National Robotics Challenge is a major robotics competition held in Marion, Ohio every spring. They have a wide range of sumo robot classes based on size and the age of the students who build them.

NRC Website

Robochallenge

Robochallenge is an international robotics competition held in Romania. It is the biggest competition in Europe and it has events for 6 different classes of sumo robots.

Robochallenge Website

FSI All Japan Robot-Sumo Tournament

The FSI All Japan Robot-Sumo Tournament is the original sumo robot competition held in Tokyo, Japan every December.
